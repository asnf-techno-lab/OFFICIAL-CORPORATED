Our Vision & Mission

History & Foundation

ASNF Techno Lab, founded on 2026/03/20 by M. Nabil and Gemini AI, was established in Aceh, Lhokseumawe City, with the capital of a Mid-Range Device (Infinix Hot 50 Pro Plus) and a Dell Latitude e527 Laptop.

My goal in creating this company was initially focused on creating root modules along with a web portal to download them. However, wanting to expand the ecosystem, I created a pioneering venture between ASNF Techno Lab and AnXin Technology Project. AnXin Technology Project operates as a sub-brand of the AnXin A.I. ecosystem, fully managed and operated by ASNF Techno Lab.
